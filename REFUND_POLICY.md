## Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with Reflexia. If you're not happy with your purchase, we offer fair and transparent refund options.

## SUBSCRIPTION PAYMENTS (Monthly/Annual Pro)

### Cancellation
- ✅ **Cancel anytime** - No questions asked
- ✅ **Cancel through** your account settings or Stripe Customer Portal
- ✅ **Access continues** until the end of your current billing period
- ✅ **No partial refunds** for unused time in the current month/year

### How to Cancel
1. Go to Settings → Manage Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. OR: Use the Stripe Customer Portal link in your receipt email

### After Cancellation
- Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period
- After expiry, your account reverts to the Free tier
- All your data remains intact (reflections, CPD records)
- You can resubscribe anytime

### No Refunds For
- ❌ Partial months or years already paid
- ❌ Subscriptions cancelled before renewal (you keep access until period ends)
- ❌ Changed your mind after using Pro features extensively
