## Pocket-Sized Professional Standards Guide

**XP Cost:** 1,500 XP (printable set) OR 35,000 XP (laminated physical cards shipped)
**Format:** Business card sized (85mm ร 55mm) - fits in lanyard/pocket
**Quantity:** 25 cards (4 pillars + key scenarios)
**Target:** UK Registered Nurses

---

## CARD SET OVERVIEW

**The 4 Pillars of The Code:**
1. **Prioritize People** (Standards 1-5) - 5 cards
2. **Practice Effectively** (Standards 6-12) - 7 cards
3. **Preserve Safety** (Standards 13-15) - 3 cards
4. **Promote Professionalism** (Standards 16-25) - 10 cards

**Total:** 25 cards covering all critical scenarios
