- **Padding:** 15mm from all edges

## Usage Instructions (Include in Package)

### How to Use Your Reflection Cards

**Daily Draw:**
Pull one card each morning. Carry it as your reflection prompt for the day. Journal about it before bed.

**Suit Focus:**
Choose a suit based on what you need:
- â™¥ï¸ Hearts when you need emotional connection
- â™¦ï¸ Diamonds when you need to learn
- â™£ï¸ Clubs when you need motivation
- â™ ï¸ Spades when facing challenges

**Team Reflection:**
Each team member draws a card. Take 2 minutes to reflect individually, then share insights for 5 minutes.

**Weekly Deep Dive:**
Draw a card on Monday. Spend the week deeply exploring that question. Document insights Friday.

**Playing Cards:**
Yes, they're real playing cards! Use for:
- Poker/card games
- Icebreaker activities
- Random selection in meetings
- Team building exercises

**Joker Power:**
When you draw a Joker, it overrides everything else. Complete the challenge within 24 hours.
