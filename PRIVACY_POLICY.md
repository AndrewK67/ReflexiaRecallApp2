# Privacy Policy - Reflexia

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Reflexia ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

**Key Principle: Your Data Stays Yours**
- Reflexia stores your reflections and data **locally in your browser**
- We do NOT have access to your personal reflections or CPD records
- You control and own all your data

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally (Browser Storage)
**What we store on YOUR device:**
- Reflections and journal entries
- CPD activity records
- User preferences and settings
- Holodeck exercise responses
- Gamification progress (achievements, streaks)
- Manual CPD records you create

**How it's stored:**
- HTML5 localStorage in your browser
- Data never leaves your device unless YOU export it
- Clearing browser data will delete this information

**We do NOT collect or transmit this data to our servers**

### 2.2 Data We Collect (Analytics & Advertising)

#### A. Usage Analytics (Anonymous)
We may collect anonymous usage data:
- Pages visited within the App
- Features used (reflection models, CPD tools)
- Time spent in the App
- Device type and browser
- General location (country/city level only)

**Purpose:** Improve the App, understand feature usage

#### B. Advertising Data (Third-Party)
**Google AdSense (or similar networks) may collect:**
- IP address
- Browser cookies
- Device identifiers
- Browsing behavior for targeted ads
- Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)

**Purpose:** Display relevant advertisements

#### C. Optional AI Features
**If you use Google Gemini AI features:**
- You provide your own API key
- Your prompts are sent to Google's servers
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
- We do NOT store or see your AI interactions

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Your reflections or journal entries
- ❌ Your CPD records or evidence
- ❌ Personal health information
- ❌ Professional registration numbers
- ❌ Identifiable patient information
- ❌ Contents of your exported files

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection)

### 6.1 Right to Access
- Access all data stored locally (via browser DevTools or export feature)
- Request information about analytics data we collect

### 6.2 Right to Deletion
- Delete local data: Clear browser storage
- Request deletion of analytics data: Contact us

### 6.3 Right to Portability
- Export your data using built-in export features (CSV, text)
- Data is yours to take elsewhere

### 6.4 Right to Object
- Opt-out of personalized advertising
- Opt-out of analytics cookies
- Use ad blockers (though this affects our revenue)

### 6.5 Right to Rectification
- Edit your reflections and CPD records directly in the App
- You have full control over your local data

### 6.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Revoke cookie consent at any time
- Stop using the App if you don't agree with data practices

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Local Data Security
- Stored in browser localStorage (client-side only)
- Subject to your device security
- Use device passwords/encryption for protection
- No transmission to our servers

**We recommend:**
- Strong device passwords
- Device encryption
- Regular backups of exported data
- Logging out of shared devices

### 7.2 No Server-Side Storage
- We do NOT store your reflections on servers
- No cloud backup (unless you manually upload exports)
- Your data stays on your device

### 7.3 Exported Data
- Exports (CSV, text) are generated locally
- You control where exports are saved
- Transmitted data (if emailed) uses standard encryption
- **Be careful** sharing sensitive CPD exports

## 8. Children's Privacy

- Reflexia is NOT intended for children under 16
- We do not knowingly collect data from children
- If you believe a child has used the App, contact us

## 9. International Data Transfers
